About

BIHAR RUBBER COMPANY LIMITED (BRC Ltd.) is India's leading waterproofing company with over 41 years of indigenous leadership. BRC Ltd. is an ISO 9001 - 2008 certified company and is the manufacturer of products like Raincoat, Rainsuit, Gumboot, Air Pillow, Hospital Sheeting, Baby Mat, Water Bed, etc. under the famous brand DUCKBACK (as Registered Permitted User). The company has also recently introduced some specialised rainwear and footwear under the brand name of BRC. The company manufactures products like school bags, trolley bags, laptop bags, rucksack and baby mat under the brands of CRUISER, TRAVELL STYLE, TRAVELL COMPANION, TRAVELL EXECUTIVE and MOTHERS LAP and all kind of complimentary and promotional items. With its corporate office in Kolkata and an integrated plant at Ranchi, Jharkhand, the company has pioneered and continues to be the leading manufacturer and marketer of a versatile range of protective gear and rubberised consumer durables. We are privileged to be one of the most recognized and trusted name in country today. The company has a strong sales and distribution network with over 1000 active dealers catering directly to retail market and institutions at national level. A well equipped Research & Development center is constantly developing new applications and innovative products. Bihar Rubber Company Limited is India’s “FIRST” waterproofing company to obtain ISI mark for its rain suits and rain coats. The company has obtained ISI Licenses – ISI: 5915 for ST rain coats and ISI: 6110 for DT rain coats and is supplying these products to government, semi government and even to private sector organizations.
